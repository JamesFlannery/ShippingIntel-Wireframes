Executive Summary

Gulf Coast Ammonia Plant (GCA) has executed its inaugural export cargo on Yara's Dancing Brave, loading 1.3M t/y capacity ammonia at Texas City for delivery to Norway (Glomfjord), triggering immediate supply pressure and the fourth consecutive monthly price decline in global ammonia markets.

Key Takeaways

  • Tampa contract prices drop $25/mt CFR to $435/mt
  • Fourth consecutive monthly decline in ammonia pricing
  • GCA plant adds 1.3M t/y capacity to global supply
  • USG-Europe trade lane established with freight advantage
